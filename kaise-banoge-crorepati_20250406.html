Crorepati

Who wants to become a crorepati?

What does it mean to be a crorepati?

Person A

Person A earns INR 50,000/- per month

pays house loan EMI of 20,000 for a house worth 20L

pays car loan EMI of 5,000 for a car worth 5L

pays children school fees of 2000

spends 10,000 on groceries

spends 5,000 on utilities (phone, electricty, gas, etc)

spends 3,000 on dining and entertainment

He is left with 5,000

Person B

Person B earns INR 2,50,000/- per month

pays house loan EMI of 1,00,000 for a house worth 1Cr

pays car loan EMI of 25,000 for a car worth 25L

pays children school fees of 20,000

spends 30,000 on groceries

spends 15,000 on utilities (phone, electricty, gas, etc)

spends 30,000 on dining and entertainment

He is left with 20,000

Time to become Crorepati

Person A

100,00,000 / 5,000 / 12 = 166.66 years

Person B

100,00,000 / 20,000 / 12 = 41.66 years

Person C

Same as person B, but invests in SIP

at 10% compound interest

How much time will it take to reach 1Cr?

16 years, 6 months

Remember, Person B needed 41.66 years

That's the power of investing!

Person D

Same as person B, but invests 30,000

How much time will it take to reach 1Cr?

13 years, 6 months

Imagine if your parents would have done a SIP for you when you were born

You would have already become a Crorepati!

Better late than never

Lessons

Invest

But you can only invest that which you save, so

Increase savings

But Savings = Income - Expenses, so

Increase income (invest in self education)

Reduce expenses

Invest regularly

Start early

Practical next steps

Educate your parents and help them save and invest

Create an investment account for yourself

Save and invest your own pocket money / gifts (birthday, prizes, etc)

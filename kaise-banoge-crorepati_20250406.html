The story of 2 people A and B

  • They both passed from same school in same year
    • A got less marks in 12th and did engineering from a small collge
    • B got good marks in 12th and did engineering from a premier collge
  • A got job in a small firm and earns 50,000/- per month
  • B got job in a MNC and earns 2,50,000/- per month

A

  • earns INR 50,000/- per month
  • pays house loan EMI of 22,000 for a house worth 22L
  • pays car loan EMI of 5,000 for a car worth 5L
  • spends 10,000 on groceries
  • spends 5,000 on utilities (phone, electricty, gas, etc)
  • spends 3,000 on dining and entertainment!!!
  • How much is he left with?

5,000/-

B1

  • earns INR 2,50,000/- per month
  • pays house loan EMI of 1,30,000 for a house worth 1.3Cr
  • pays car loan EMI of 25,000 for a car worth 25L
  • spends 30,000 on groceries
  • spends 15,000 on utilities (phone, electricty, gas, etc)
  • spends 30,000 on dining and entertainment!!!
  • How much is he left with?

20,000/-
